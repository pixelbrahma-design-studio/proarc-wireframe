Woodlem Park Private School

Full-Width Hero PhotoSchool façade or covered play area

Project Overview

Woodlem Park Private School sits in Al Jurf 3, Ajman, an area surrounded by ongoing developments and envisioned for the future. Set over two floors, the school is designed to house 4,230 students from KG to Grade 12, with a state-of-the-art auditorium, labs and library.

Client Requirements

  • — A large-capacity school (4,230 students) delivered in phases for ease of construction
  • — Separate, generous play areas for boys and girls
  • — A facility that feels safe and nurturing for young students

Challenges Faced

  • — Bringing natural light into classrooms without exposing them to harsh direct sun
  • — Phasing a large campus for continuous construction without disrupting operations
  • — Balancing a traditional, familiar school character with a modern, efficient plan

Proarc's Approach

The outer façade mimics brick cladding reminiscent of long-standing schools of the past, giving the building old-school charm. Regular geometric forms create balance and a sense of effortless grace along the exterior. Natural light is brought into classrooms through strategically placed large openings, while tents assembled over the playgrounds diffuse and refract harsh sunlight before it reaches the classrooms.

Final Outcome

A 24,214 sq.m. campus for client READ, completed in 2017 — a safe, light-filled hub for learning and growth, built in manageable phases and now a fixture of the fast-developing Al Jurf district.
