02 — Why Work With Us

At PROARC, our collaborative team of architects, engineers, MEP engineers and site engineers has shaped Ajman's skyline since 2006. We work enthusiastically, creatively and curiously, taking on projects of every type and scale — from skyscrapers to schools — with the same commitment to thoroughness and lasting impact.

Creative Environment

Work across urban masterplans, skyscrapers, schools, malls and private homes — rarely the same brief twice.

Growth Opportunities

A multidisciplinary in-house team means real cross-training across architecture, MEP and project management.

Landmark Projects

Contribute to buildings that define Ajman's skyline and are recognised across the UAE.
