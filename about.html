About PROARC
02 — Company Introduction
ImageOffice exterior, team photo, or founder portrait

Proarc, one of the leading architectural practices in the UAE, has been an integral contributor to Ajman's emerging iconic skyline since 2006. Our collaborative team of experienced architects, engineers, MEP engineers and site engineers ensure that all designs are executed with utmost thoroughness and commitment.

Over the past few years we have pioneered a sustainable approach to architecture through a strikingly wide range of work — from urban masterplans, public infrastructure and retail spaces, to offices, health-care, institutional, mixed-use, skyscraper and private house projects. Based in Ajman, we have built an international reputation, with buildings across the UAE, and we aim to create a lasting social, economic and environmental effect with everything we take on.

Founding Year: 2006
Headquarters: 13003, Ajman, United Arab Emirates
Team: A multidisciplinary in-house team of architects, engineers, MEP engineers and site engineers
