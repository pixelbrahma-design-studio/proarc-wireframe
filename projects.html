Ajman Bank Headquarters

Image / SliderFull-width hero image or slider — reflective glass façade, 3–8 photos

Ajman Bank Headquarters is a G+M+7-storey iconic corporate complex located on Al Ittihad Street, adjacent to the Etisalat building. This HQ is responsible for transactions across the globe. The building's bold, striking, reflective, inclined glass façade creates interlocking shapes and volumes that transcend the simple need for workspace — becoming an urban landmark.

Project Type: Commercial — Corporate HQ
Location: Ajman, United Arab Emirates
Status: Completed

Built-up Area: 73,071 sq. ft.
Year of Completion: 2015
Client: Ajman Bank

Scope of Work
